Abstract Submission


Submission Deadline: July 15, 2019



▶Authors are requested to electronically submit: 1) A 120-word abstract 2) A one page summary in 'pdf' or 'word' format: Download template in format.
▶Abstracts can be up to 1 page in length and must be submitted online via MTSA2019 website(https://mtsa2019.org). A review process for those who submitted abstracts will be managed by MTSA2019 Program committee.
▶Poster size should be less than A0 standard (84.1cm X 118.9 cm).
▶All speakers and poster presenters must register with their respective organizing association. All MTSA2019 speakers and poster presenters need to register via the MTSA2019 online registration system.
▶If you need any help, please contact "5th.MTSA2019@gmail.com" for assistance.
